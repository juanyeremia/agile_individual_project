My name is Juan Yeremia Yovian. I am a Masters of Information Technology student at the University of Western Australia, with a background in Industrial Engineering from Bina Nusantara University in Indonesia. My professional journey has taken me through roles in logistics, customer service, and supply chain management, giving me a strong foundation in data analysis, process improvement, and cross-functional collaboration. I am currently expanding my technical skills through my studies at UWA, with a focus on web development, software engineering, and information systems. Outside of academics, I enjoy problem solving, building systems that make everyday tasks more efficient, and continuously learning new technologies.

I am passionate about bridging the gap between my engineering and logistics background with modern technology, and I am eager to contribute to organisations that value innovation, data-driven decision making, and continuous improvement. I am currently seeking opportunities where I can apply both my technical and analytical skills in a professional setting.
